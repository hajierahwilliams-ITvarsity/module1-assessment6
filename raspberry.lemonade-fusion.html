WHAT IS IT?

A Raspberry Lemonade Fusion Smoothie is a delicious and refreshing beverage that combines the flavors of raspberries and lemonade to create a delightful and tangy treat. It is the fusion of sweet and tart flavors that makes this smoothie particularly appealing.

KEY INGREDIENTS

1 cup fresh or frozen raspberries 1/4 cup lemon juice (freshly squeezed) 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt (or dairy-free yogurt for a vegan option) 1/2 to 3/4 cup water or ice cubes (adjust to your desired consistency) 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional, for added sweetness) Lemon zest (optional, for an extra burst of lemon flavorful .
