WHAT IS IT?

Blueberries are delicious and nutritious fruits that are commonly used in various dishes, including smoothies. To make a simple and tasty blueberry smoothie, you can use the following ingredients:

KEY INGREDIENTS

1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries 1/2 to 3/4 cup plain Greek yogurt or dairy-free yogurt for a vegan option 1/2 to 3/4 cup milk (dairy or plant-based like almond milk, soy milk, etc.) 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional, for added sweetness) 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional) Ice cubes (optional, for a colder and thicker consistency)
